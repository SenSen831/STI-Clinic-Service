Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for STI Clinic services?
You can register online through the STI Clinic portal. Just click the “Online Clinical Registration” button on the homepage.
Who can access the clinic?
Medical interns, students, and community members are all welcome to use our services.
What services are offered?
We offer general consultations, laboratory tests, health education, and more. Visit the “Information about STI Clinic” section for full details.
